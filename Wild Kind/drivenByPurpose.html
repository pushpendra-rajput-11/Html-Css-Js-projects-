At WildKind, we believe every choice should protect what matters most — our companions and our planet.

Our Mission

WildKind was forged with a simple vision: all animals deserve to be treated with the same respect and care. From the billions of chickens and cows we depend on, to the cheetahs whose numbers have fallen from 100,000 to fewer than 8,000, every life matters.
But our purpose doesn’t stop at animals. We are committed to protecting the planet they call home — and the environments humans depend on, too. Every product we curate is chosen with care: strong enough to last, kind enough to reduce harm, and built to create a future where pets, wildlife, and people thrive together.
